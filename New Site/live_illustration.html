how it works

On the day I will arrive at the venue to set up half an hour early. I will need a table and somewhere with good lighting.

Guests are then able to approach me to request a portrait. I take a quick photograph of them to work from so nobody has to stand like a statue as I draw them and can enjoy the day.

I will make sure to get a photo of the newlyweds as I know it’s a busy day for you!

I display all the finished illustrations for everyone to see and guests can take their’s home at the end of the night in a compostable protective sleeve.

50 miles travel included from South London - after that there is a charge of 50p/mile.

FAQs

Where can I cover?

I am based in London, but happy to travel a few hours from there. For any bookings that fall over 1.5 hours away from my location, only the Emerald or Diamond package is available. I often visit family in the north of England so please enquire if your wedding is further afield as I may be able to make it work. I am also available for destination weddings.

What materials do I provide?

I personalise watercolour, high-quality paper with your names and wedding date on. If you already have a monogram for your wedding I can add that on too. I supply compostable protective sleeves to put the illustrations in to take home.

What if some guests don’t get an illustration who want one?

I can take photos of the guests at the event and complete them at home and send them on to the newlyweds. For larger packages I will agree beforehand how many to complete at home. Additional illustrations can be purchased after the wedding for £15 if anyone missed out.

How many guests per illustration?

I work on the basis of 2 guests per illustration, but can also illustrate guests by themselves or in groups of three. This allows as many people as possible to take home a physical illustration. For families, I can add extra children/babies if needed.
