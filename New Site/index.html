About me...

I have a degree in animation (specialising in 2D) which gave me great practice at doing hundreds or even thousands of drawings in order to make an animated short film. I’ve worked as an illustrator for the last 8 years, recently returning to working freelance after 4 years as an in-house illustrator and graphic designer. I’ve since started ‘Drawn by Chloe Hill’ and I love getting to add something really special to your events and meet lots of new people.
