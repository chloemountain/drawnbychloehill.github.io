COMMISSIONS

Looking for a unique wedding present no one else will have bought? Perhaps, you want to gift a personalised thank-you card for your bridesmaids? Or maybe a birthday or Christmas present - it doesn’t have to be anything to do with weddings! Provide me some photos to work from and I create a lovely illustration to show your loved ones how much they mean to you!

Do you know what your first anniversary is? ...It’s paper. Which alone may not be the most exciting gift, but add one of my illustrations to it and you have a really appropriate paper anniversary present! In the anniversary illustration here I created  a wintery floral border based on the couple’s wedding invitation designs, but in a colour scheme that tied in more with their outfits.

PLACE NAME CARDS 

Loving the idea of giving your guests personalised illustrations, but not so sure live illustration will fit into your day? Good news is that even if I’m booked up on your date you can still commission personalised place cards for your tables. You can opt to give them to all your guests or just for the head table. I can add your names and your wedding date to them so that your guests have a lovely way to remember your wedding. I can also add their names - or just leave them to play guess who!

Minimum order of 6. Please contact me for a quote.
